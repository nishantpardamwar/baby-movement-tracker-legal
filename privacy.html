Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26th June 2026

Baby Movement Tracker ("the App") is developed and maintained by Nishant Pardamwar ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Pregnancy Data

The App stores information you enter, including:

  • Baby movement sessions
  • Movement timestamps
  • Session notes
  • Pregnancy journal entries
  • Photos attached to journal entries (Premium feature)

By default, this information is stored locally on your device.

Google Drive Backup (Premium)

If you choose to enable cloud backup, the App requests permission to access your Google Drive App Folder. Your data is backed up only after your explicit consent. We do not access any other files in your Google Drive.

Purchase Information

Premium purchases are processed securely by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment information.

Analytics

The App may use Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used, including anonymous usage statistics.

Crash Reports

The App may use Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information to improve app stability.

Advertising

The free version of the App may display advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and other information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. Premium users do not see advertisements.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the App's features
  • Restore purchases
  • Back up your data if enabled
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Fix crashes and bugs

Third-Party Services

The App may use the following services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Drive API (optional)

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Delete your data at any time from within the App (if supported).
  • Uninstall the App to remove locally stored data.
  • Disable Google Drive backup.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
